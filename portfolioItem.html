Overview

The Mystery man walks out of the metro station to meet his client, Mr. Donut. The deal is about something inside the suitcase. Viewers will follow the suitcase, experiencing the secret deal from the Old Port to Champlain College. At the end of the video, viewers should have their expectations of what is inside the suitcase and what will happen next.

Technology

We use GoPro Fusion for our project. There are five scenes, each of them is around 30 seconds to 40 seconds. We use lavalier microphones to record the main characters' phone calls and conversations. We use the internal microphone of GoPro to record ambisonic audio and plan to edit them in post-production. There are other audio sources, including the street noise, a jogger running by, and the sound of the door opening and closing.
