Project AR Pet Fish

Augmented Reality

Overview

This application lets users interact with their pet fish through their device screen. Users scan the real-world environment and detect the plane. Once the plane detection is done, users click the screen to place the pet fish. Pet fish will be slowly swimming in place until users give it instructions.

Features

Users will touch the screen to interact with the pet fish. With one finger touch, pet fish will swim to the designated location. With two fingers touch, pet fish will play dead. With three fingers touching the screen, pet fish will jump out of water; With four fingers touch, users can place seed at the location, and pet fish will follow the seed and eat it immediately.

Technology

Augmented Reality; Unity 3D; ARcore;
