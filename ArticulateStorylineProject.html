Project English Pronunciation Lesson

Articulate Storyline

Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  1. Recognize the target sound /i:/ in the example words in the vocabulary of this lesson.
  2. Pronounce the six words in the vocabulary with seeing the correct spelling.
  3. Classify letter patterns (ee & ea) that pronounce the target sound /i:/ in the target words in the vocabulary.
  4. Recognize the six words in the vocabulary with the objects presented in image.
Target Sound: /i:/
Target Vocabulary: sheep, leak, cheeks, peel, bean, leave.

Functions & Components
